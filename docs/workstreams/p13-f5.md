## Mission

Build F5, the read-only compliance auditor. F5 reviews signed audit artifacts,
detects deterministic rate-limit, budget-pressure, route, purpose, and policy
coverage findings, and emits typed compliance findings without mutating runtime
behavior.

## Constraints

- F5 is read-only.
- F5 cannot block, mutate, or suppress audit events.
- P13 is deterministic. The prompt file exists for future optional explanation
  or anomaly review, but live model calls must not decide hard compliance
  findings.
- The rate-limit threshold is configurable. Defaults are `max_queries=5` and
  `window_seconds=60`; the sixth query inside that window triggers a finding.
- `rate_limit_triggered=True` requires a `kind="rate_limit"` finding.
- HIGH or CRITICAL findings force `human_review_required=True`.
- No raw customer names in finding details.

## Acceptance

- The sixth query from one investigator inside the default 60-second window
  emits a rate-limit finding.
- The query limit and window are configurable for demos and tests.
- Budget pressure, missing Lobster Trap verdicts, and route or purpose
  anomalies emit typed findings.
- Vague or invalid purpose signals can produce a human-review finding.
- Normal canonical-flow audit artifacts do not over-flag.
- Focused tests pass.
